# Archive Da Duoc Don Dep

Thu muc `archive/` da duoc xoa noi dung cu de giai phong dung luong va giam tai cho IDE/OpenCode.

Noi dung da xoa truoc do chu yeu la du lieu va ma nguon khong con phuc vu luong chinh cua du an, gom:

- `VAIPE_Full/`: bo du lieu cu rat lon, khong con phu hop dinh dang don thuoc thuc te hien tai.
- `weights/`: cac trong so cu khong con la trong tam cua he thong hien tai.
- `output/`: ket qua dau ra cu da luu tru.
- `YOLO11-Seg-Label-Detector-main/`: cong cu gan nhan da dung xong.
- `notebooks/`, `legacy/`, `scripts/`, `deprecated_gcn/`: ma nghien cuu va thanh phan da loai bo.
- `plan_bin/`, `root_clutter/`: tep va thu muc tap hop tam, khong con can thiet cho luong chinh.

Ly do don dep:

- giam dung luong workspace;
- giam kha nang IDE/indexer quet nham noi dung cu;
- giu repo gon hon cho phan phat trien app va bao cao hien tai.

Neu can khoi phuc, chi nen khoi phuc nhung phan thuc su can cho viec doi chieu lich su hoac nghien cuu bo sung.
